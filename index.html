Abstract

We introduce a novel method for deepfake video detection that utilizes pixel‚Äëwise temporal frequency spectra. Unlike previous approaches that stack 2D frame‚Äëwise spatial frequency spectra, we extract pixel‚Äëwise temporal frequency by performing a 1D Fourier transform on the time axis per pixel, effectively identifying temporal artifacts.
